About TaxiApp

What is TaxiApp?

TaxiApp is a modern ride-booking and taxi management platform designed for both customers and drivers. It allows users to book rides in real-time, while offering drivers an intuitive dashboard to manage rides, shifts, and earnings. The system also includes a web-based admin panel for taxi entrepreneurs to monitor business operations.

Purpose

The main goal of TaxiApp is to digitalize and simplify the taxi business by providing:

  • Real-time ride booking for customers
  • Smart ride tracking and pricing for drivers
  • Administrative tools for entrepreneurs
  • Secure user authentication with different roles (Admin, Driver, User)

Meet the Founder

TaxiApp was created by a passionate developer named Bashkim Grepi, with a vision to streamline the taxi service industry using modern web and mobile technologies. With a background in software development and an eye for user experience, the founder continues to build and improve the app to meet real-world needs.
